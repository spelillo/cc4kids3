📘 Shep Learns a Lesson

Chevy, Goldie, and their new friend, Toby, a quiet and shy little dog, were happily playing together in the park. Suddenly, they are interrupted by Shep: a big, loud, intimidating dog that stirs up trouble, and frightens Toby. As Chevy and Goldie unite to help their new little friend, there is a twist of events, and a revelation unfolds. How will the dogs work through their troubles with Shep? Will Shep finally learn his lesson?

Author: Christine M. Pelillo

Illustrator: Elisa Lorraine Yeto

Published: April 5, 2024

Language: English

Length: 30 pages

ISBN-13: 979-8765250679

Dimensions: 8.5 x 0.08 x 8.5 inches

👩‍🏫 About the Author

Christine M. Pelillo is a passionate children's author who brings important life lessons to young readers through the adventures of lovable dogs. Her stories focus on empathy, teamwork, and kindness, all wrapped in tail-wagging fun!
