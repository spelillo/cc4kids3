Shep Learns A Lesson

"Shep Learns A Lesson" came to life as a passion project when my first rescue dog, Chevy, passed away. As I was brainstorming ways to hold onto the precious memories with Chevy, writing a book became the perfect answer! Throughout the story, four dogs navigate through big emotions and even an injury, and figure out how to become friends through courage, kindness, empathy and facing their fears.

The Canine Crew

🐶 Meet Chevy 🐾

Chevy is the leader of the Canine Crew with a nose for adventure and a heart full of care! She's always ready to help others—especially when someone is learning an important life lesson.

  • 🧠 Thinking before acting
  • ❤️ Being a good friend
  • 🎯 Helping others learn from mistakes

Chevy believes that every day is a chance to learn something new—and to be your best self!

🐕 Meet Goldie the Golden Retriever ✨

Goldie is a gentle, golden-hearted dog who shines with kindness and wisdom. She’s like the big sister of the group—calm, caring, and always ready with a helpful word or a comforting nudge.

  • 💛 Helping others solve problems
  • 🌼 Playing gently with younger animals
  • 🧠 Teaching by example — she shows how to do the right thing, even when it's hard!

Goldie’s Motto:
“Kindness always leads the way.”

💙 Meet Toby the Timid Mutt 🐾

Toby is a sweet, gentle pup with big eyes and a bigger heart. He’s a little shy and sometimes nervous, but he’s learning every day that it’s okay to be scared—and that brave doesn't mean loud, it means trying your best, even when it’s hard.

Sometimes Toby needs a moment to think or a safe space to calm down—and that’s totally okay! He reminds us that everyone has their own way of being brave.

  • 🧸 Quiet corners and soft blankets
  • 📚 Listening to stories (he's a great listener!)
  • 🌈 Friends who help him feel safe and included

🐕 Meet Shep! 🌟

Shep is a lively, curious dog who’s still learning how to be the best friend he can be. Sometimes Shep gets a little too excited or forgets to think before he acts — but that’s okay!

  • 🎾 Playing with his friends
  • 🐾 Exploring new places
  • 💡 Figuring out how to do better every day

When Shep realizes he’s made a mistake, he takes a deep breath and listens to his friends like Goldie, Chevy, and Toby. With their help, he learns important lessons about kindness, patience, and being a good friend.

About Us

- Miss Christine, Children's Author & Educator -

Welcome to the world of storytelling and education! I am dedicated to creating engaging and impactful content for children, using my experience as an educator and a passion for writing. Through my books, I aim to inspire young minds and foster a love for reading and learning.

More About Miss Christine

I am a passionate educator, author, philanthropist in action, and active volunteer for the TN HABIT Program (Human-Animal Bond in Tennessee), along with my beloved rescue dog, Finn. I am an ardent supporter for the well-being of both children and dogs alike. Educating children for over 30 years gave me the insight I needed to fulfill a lifelong dream: to write & publish a children's book!
